SKYXX: An Alternative Band

Skyxx is an alternative band that emerged from the vibrant community of CvSU Musikeros,
the official music organization of Cavite State University – De Las Alas Campus. Formed by passionate student
musicians, the group quickly found its sound in the dynamic blend of emotional lyricism and raw instrumental energy.
Their music reflects both individuality and unity, expressing the stories and experiences that
connect them as artists and friends.

The band is composed of four talented members: Rosemarie as the soulful Vocalist and Rhythm Guitarist,
Eric as the electrifying Lead Guitarist, Carl as the steady and groovy Bassist, and Eugene as the energetic
Drummer who drives the heartbeat of the group. Together, they bring to life the spirit of alternative music
through their unique chemistry and creative expression. Skyxx continues to evolve, aiming to inspire others
through their sound and passion for music.
